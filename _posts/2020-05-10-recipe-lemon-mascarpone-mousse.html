I know, all good things come to those who wait and a real mousse contains raw eggs that need setting, but here's a recipe for when you don't have the patience. I just grabbed some leftovers from the fridge and was quite pleased with the result: a zingy and fresh, yet sweet, soft and silky mousse that is quite airy! The recipe is easy peasy lemon squeezy, takes less than 15 minutes and you can enjoy the mousse instantly - if you haven't licked the entire bowl already.

Recipe: Cheat's Lemon Mascarpone Mousse

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 125 g / 1/2 cup of mascarpone
  • 200 g / 4/5 cup of whipping cream
  • zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 100 g / 1/3 cup of  sugar
  • mint, for garnish

Method

  1. Put the mascarpone in a bowl and add 75 g (6 tbsp) of sugar and the juice of 1 lemon. Beat the mixture, until you are left with a thick, glistening yogurt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whip the whipping cream with the remaining 25 g (2 tbsp) of sugar.
  3. When the cream is whipped, add the zest of half a lemon.
  4. Add the whipped cream to the mascarpone mixture scoop by scoop. Gently fold each scoop of cream into the mascarpone mix before adding the next.
  5. Once the cream and mascarpone are well mixed, scoop it into dessert cups.
  6. Grate over some more lemon zest and put a mint leaf on top for garnish.
