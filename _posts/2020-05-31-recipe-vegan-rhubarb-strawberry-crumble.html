This vegan strawberry and rhubarb crumble makes for an excellent fresh dessert because of the mint. The recipe is very simple and quick and the crumble is low sugar, so even on a diet you can enjoy it guilt free (as you always should).

Recipe: Vegan Rhubarb & Strawberry Crumble with Mint, Rosewater, Cardamom and Cinnamon

Ingredients (serves 2 - 4)

    For the filling:
  • 200 g of rhubarb, cut in slices
    •
  • 150 g of strawberries, in quarters (or halves if small)
    •
  • 2 tbsp of sugar
    •
  • 1 tbsp of rosewater
    •
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
    •
  • 2 tsp cardamom
    •
  • leaves of 1 stalk of spearmint, chopped
    •
    For the crumble:
  • 40 g wheat flour
    •
  • 40 g of rolled oats
    •
  • 1 big tbsp g of light brown sugar (Dutch: lichte basterdsuiker)
    •
  • 1 big tbsp of coconut oil, solid but soft
    •
  • a pinch of salt
    •
  • a pinch of cinnamon
    •
Method
  1. Preheat the oven to 175 C. Grease a small baking tin or casserole with some oil.
  2. Mix all the ingredients for the filling in a large bowl and transfer to the baking tin.
  3. Mix the ingredients for the topping with two forks, until you have a chunky crumble.
  4. Transfer the batter to the baking tin.
  5. Bake in the oven for 35 minutes, until nicely browned.
  6. Serve warm or cold.
