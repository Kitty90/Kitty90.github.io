Recipe: Vegan Rhubarb Pie

Ingredients (serves 8)
Wet ingredients

  • 240 ml of almond milk
    •
  • 4 tbsp of lemon juice
    •
  • 60 ml of coconut oil
    •
  • 0.5 tsp almond essence
    •
  • 125 g caster sugar
    •
Dry ingredients
  • 220 g flour
    •
  • 80g almond meal
    •
  • 2 tsp baking powder
    •
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom seeds
    •
  • a pinch of salt
    •
Topping

  • 150 g rhubarb, in slices
    •
  • 1 tsp rosewater
    •
  • 2 tbsp sugar
    •
  • 50 g of peeled and unsalted pistachios for garnish
    •
Method
  1. Preheat the oven at 170 C. Grease the pie tin with some oil.
  2. Mix all the wet ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Sieve in all the dry ingredients and mix carefully. Do not overmix the batter, as that would make the pastry tough.
  4. Transfer the batter to the baking tin.
  5. Mix the rhubarb slices with the rosewater and 2 tbsp of sugar.
  6. Sprinkle some extra almond flour on top of the batter before placing the pieces on top. This will prevent the rhubarb from sinking into the batter too much.
  7. Place the pieces of rhubarb on top of the batter.
  8. Bake for 55-60 minutes, until nicely browned and a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Let the cake cool down and then finish it off by sprinkling over chopped pistachios and caster sugar.
