Otherwise, it won't develop a good flavour.

Recipe: Lentil and Parsnip Stir-Fry


Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 500 g parsnip
  • 125 g chestnut mushrooms
  • 125 g oyster mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 cup of lentils
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 mushroom stock cube
  • 5 sprigs of fresh curly leaf parsley
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Method

  1. Rinse the lentils and bring 500 ml water to a boil in a pot.
  2. Add the lentils, stock cube and bay leaf to the water. Cook for 15 minutes.
  3. Peel the parsnip and cut into dices.
  4. Heat up a lug of oil in a frying pan. Chop 2 cloves of garlic and onion and add to the frying pan together with the parsnip cubes.
  5. Meanwhile, cut the (chestnut)  mushrooms into quarters and tear the oyster mushrooms into slivers.
  6. Add the mushrooms to the onion and garlic and season with salt and pepper. Bake until cooked, but not mushy.
  7. Drain the lentils and add them to the mushrooms. Mix well.
  8. Chop some fresh parsley and blend through the mixture. Scoop the lentils and mushrooms on plates and sprinkle over some remaining parsley.
